Othello Game

Current Player: Black

Rules of Othello

  • The game is played on an 8x8 board.
  • Players take turns placing their discs on the board.
  • A move consists of placing a disc in a position that outflanks the opponent's discs.
  • Outflanked discs are flipped to the current player's color.
  • The game ends when no more moves are possible, and the player with the most discs on the board wins.
